Book Review Blog - Site Plan

Site Name

The Literary Haven

I selected this name to clearly represent the site's purpose of providing detailed book reviews and being a haven for readers.

Site Purpose

The purpose of this site is to provide detailed book reviews and allow readers to suggest books for future reviews, while also finding some good readings for themselves.

Scenarios

1. What are the most highly rated books reviewed on this site?

2. How can I suggest a book for review?

Typography
